About Us – PRINTX

Welcome to PRINTX, your one-stop solution for all printing needs! Our journey officially started on 16th December 2024, with a dream to make the printing experience simpler, faster, and more affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

In today’s busy world, going to a shop for printing work consumes a lot of time, energy, and extra money. PRINTX was created to solve this problem. Through our online platform, you can place all your printing orders from the comfort of your home. This means you save time, money, and energy, while still receiving professional-quality printing services at your fingertips.

Our Story

I am Omkar Savita Prakash Kamble, the founder of PRINTX and a proud student of The D.G. Ruparel College, Mahim. What began as a small idea has grown into a bigger dream — to take the printing industry online and make it more accessible for everyone.

Why Choose PRINTX?

  • Easy and hassle-free online ordering process
  • Save time, money, and energy
  • High-quality printing at affordable prices
  • Customer-focused and reliable service

At PRINTX, we believe that printing should not be a hassle. Our mission is to make your printing experience smooth, cost-effective, and stress-free.
