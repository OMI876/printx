Return & Exchange Center

Welcome to the PRINTX Return & Exchange Center.
We value your trust and aim to make the process easy for products like Stickers, Photoframes, and T-Shirts.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Product must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • Customised items (with personal photos or text) are non-returnable unless defective.
  • Refunds will be processed via UPI within 5–7 working days after inspection.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges allowed within 7 days of delivery.
  • Only size/color issues for T-Shirts can be exchanged.
  • Stickers and Photoframes can only be exchanged if they are defective or misprinted.
  • Customer pays return shipping for exchanges unless the item is defective or wrong.
